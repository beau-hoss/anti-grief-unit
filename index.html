The Anti Grief Unit is an elite law enforcement division operating under the Los Santos Police Department. We are a structured, GTA Online roleplay clan built around discipline, teamwork, and protecting the server from griefers and toxic players.

Our Mission

What the Anti-Grief Unit Does

Every officer in the Anti Grief Unit serves a purpose. Here's what we do to keep Los Santos safe.

🛡️

Anti-Grief Response

Rapid deployment to active grief incidents. Our officers identify griefers, document evidence, and coordinate a response to neutralize the threat and protect civilians.

🚔

Server Patrol

Regular patrol sessions ensure a visible police presence across Los Santos. Officers log their patrol hours and respond to civilian reports in real time.

📡

Dispatch Operations

Coordinated radio dispatch keeps all on-duty officers connected and responsive. We use 10-codes and structured callouts for professional communication.

📋

Incident Reporting

All arrests, warnings, and incidents are formally documented with case numbers. Our records provide accountability and a paper trail for enforcement decisions.

🏅

Officer Development

Cadets receive structured training and mentorship. Officers can earn commendations, climb the rank ladder, and develop real leadership skills within the unit.

🚫

Blacklist Management

Known griefers are tracked in a community blacklist. When blacklisted players are spotted, all on-duty units receive an immediate alert to respond accordingly.

Code of Conduct

Rules & Regulations

All officers are expected to uphold these standards at all times — in and out of patrol. Violations are taken seriously.

01

Zero Tolerance for Griefing

Any officer caught griefing civilians, teammates, or other players will face immediate discharge. We are the anti-grief unit — we lead by example.

02

Respect the Chain of Command

Orders from superior officers are to be followed without argument during active patrol. Disputes are to be raised through proper channels after the fact.

03

Maintain Professional Conduct

Officers represent the LSPD at all times. Toxic behavior, harassment, or disrespect towards any player — ally or civilian — will result in disciplinary action.

04

Patrol Hour Requirements

All active officers must meet their weekly patrol hour quota of 12 hours within a 7 day time frame. Failure to log minimum hours without prior notice may result in a warning or demotion.

05

Proper Uniform & Callsign

Officers must wear the approved LSPD uniform during official patrols and use their assigned callsign when communicating on the radio or in dispatch.

06

Document All Incidents

Arrests, warnings, and incidents must be formally logged. Undocumented enforcement action is not recognized and officers may face review for unreported incidents.

07

Use of Force Policy

Lethal force is a last resort. Officers must attempt verbal warnings and non-lethal options before escalating. Excessive force reports will be investigated by command.

08

Loyalty & Confidentiality

Internal unit matters, blacklists, and personnel files are confidential. Sharing sensitive LSPD information with outside parties is grounds for immediate discharge.
